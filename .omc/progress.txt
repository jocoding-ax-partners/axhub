### Learnings

- 100-row stratified subset is the right hand-curation budget. 331 = too dense for quality; 20 = too narrow for confidence. 100 = 1 person, 1 day, honest predictions.
- Margin holding identical (+40pp) at 20-row vs 100-row scope is a strong signal that design genuinely scales — NOT proof (single-curator bias remains), but supportive.
- Architect false-negative pattern: `find . -name X` may skip hidden dirs (.omc/, .claude-plugin/) on some shells. Always verify file existence with `ls .omc/` direct path before assuming missing.
- Score.ts behavior on null expected_cmd_pattern: passes if no axhub command fired (negative case correctness). This compresses delta on negative-heavy corpora — acceptable for current 14-row negative subset, may need re-evaluation if NEG fraction grows.
